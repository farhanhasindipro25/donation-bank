Community Service Agenda

The prime target of donation bank is to provide help to distressed people on a very basic level.
Our plan includes three entities namely, providing food, shelter and education to those in need.

Food for family

Necessary groceries are provided to the families in need. Those who don't have cooking facilities also get cooked meals that we buy from donations.

Shelter for a family

Families or stray children living in rural areas that don't have a place to stay are welcomed to the nearest branch of Donation Bank. They get beds, pillows, blankets, foods in well-maintained rooms.

Education for a child

Free education is provided to the children taking shelter from Donation bank, and also to children outside, so that they do not fall behind in staying educated and find a well-paid work to take care after their families by the time they are adults.
